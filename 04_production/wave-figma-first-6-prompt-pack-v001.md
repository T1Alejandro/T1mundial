# Wave Figma First 6 Prompt Pack

## Uso

Usar cada still como referencia visual estricta. El objetivo no es reimaginar la toma: es darle movimiento cinematografico sin romper composicion, identidad, color, vestuario ni riesgo legal.

Para transiciones entre stills, usar `camera-animation-bible-36-v001.md`.

## Global Style

```text
Cinematic Mexican commerce and football parallel world, grounded realism, authorial film language, soft analog highlight rolloff, fine grain, lifted blacks, textured skin, fabric, metal, concrete, grass, dust and haze, natural light first, practical light second, restrained warm-gold and cool-shadow palette, red accent #DA3B2B only where present in the reference, emotional but not patriotic, never stock advertising.
```

## Global Motion

```text
Animate ONLY from the provided still reference as a strict keyframe anchor. Do not redesign framing, staging, blocking or lens choice. Motion must emerge from the still itself.

Preserve composition, camera height, focal length feeling, perspective lines, blocking, lighting direction, color balance, subject proportions, environmental geometry, wardrobe and identity.

Allow only subtle camera drift, controlled dolly, atmospheric motion, cloth motion, breathing, natural light fluctuation and shallow focus transitions. Keep movement elegant, grounded and realistic. Do not invent story elements.

Temporal realism: natural human timing, grounded inertia, physically plausible movement, no hyperactive motion, no speed ramps, no floating limbs, no AI morph transitions.

Texture continuity: consistent analog grain structure across all shots, no digital sharpening, no temporal denoise smoothing, no plastic temporal smear.
```

## Orden De Prueba En Wave/Figma

1. Cargar los 6 stills como referencias.
2. Crear un canvas con `Global Style`, `Global Motion` y `Global Negative`.
3. Correr una version `v001` por shot, sin variaciones todavia.
4. Revisar continuidad en secuencia 01-06.
5. Solo despues hacer `v002` en las tomas que fallen.
6. Guardar exports y notas por toma en `04_production/ai-generations/motion-tests/shot-##/`.
