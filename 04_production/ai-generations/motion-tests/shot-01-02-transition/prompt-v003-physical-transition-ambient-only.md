Prompt:

```text
Usa la primera imagen como primer fotograma exacto y la segunda imagen como fotograma final exacto de referencia. Crea una sola toma cinematografica continua entre ambas imagenes.

REGLA PRINCIPAL:
No uses fade, semi fade, dissolve, semi dissolve, crossfade, opacity blend, hard cut, black frame, white flash, montage transition or generic transition. Do not hide the transition with a fading background or semi-transparent background blend. The transition must happen through physical camera movement, occlusion, matching shapes, light, floor lines, walls and material transformation.

OBJETIVO:
Transformar la tienda abriendose en el tunel del estadio mediante un match fisico de pies, piso, paredes y luz. La toma debe sentirse como una camara real que viaja de un espacio al otro, no como dos videos disueltos.

CAMARA:
Start in the exact first frame. Low frontal centered camera facing the metal shutter. The camera tilts and travels downward from the hands and silhouette to the legs and feet of the person opening the shutter. As the camera reaches the floor, use the floor lines, vertical walls, warm light beam and metal/concrete textures as a natural wipe/occlusion. The floor of the shop physically transforms into the stadium tunnel floor. Continue into the exact last frame as a low ground-level dolly behind the fictional football player's legs.

TRANSICION FISICA:
Use the lower shutter edge, the person's legs, floor shadows, wall lines and dust as occluding elements. Match the feet position and floor perspective from image 1 into the player's feet and tunnel perspective from image 2. The background should not dissolve; it should be replaced by camera movement through occlusion and matching geometry.

ACCION:
The shutter rises subtly. Dust moves in the warm light. The camera descends to the feet. The shop floor and tunnel floor align. The football player's legs continue walking calmly toward the strong backlight. Keep movement controlled and elegant.

ESTILO:
Contemporary cinematic realism, warm dawn light, concrete and metal texture, lifted blacks, analog highlight rolloff, fine film grain, shallow depth of field, realistic motion blur, authorial film look, not stock, not advertising.

CONTINUIDAD:
Preserve the exact composition and golden shutter light of the first frame at the beginning. Preserve the exact tunnel, low camera, football legs, red socks, white shorts, green fictional jersey and strong backlight of the last frame at the end. Uniforms and shoes must have no logos or brands.

AUDIO:
No audio. Audio off. Do not generate music, score, beat, voiceover, announcer, crowd chant or trailer sound. If the tool forces audio, use only natural ambience: subtle metal shutter movement, soft room tone, distant tunnel/stadium ambience, low footsteps.

PROHIBIDO:
No fade out. No fade in. No semi fade. No dissolve. No semi dissolve. No crossfade. No opacity blend. No semi-transparent background blend. No background dissolve. No hard cut. No black frame. No white flash. No generic transition. No music. No soundtrack. No trailer score. No montage effect. No abstract morphing. No melting bodies. No new location. No new people. No readable text. No logos. No brands. No official sports identity.

NEGATIVO:
sin texto, sin captions, sin subtitulos, sin labels, sin titulos, sin marcas de agua, sin logos, sin marcas comerciales, sin marcas deportivas, sin uniformes oficiales, sin equipos reales, sin seleccion nacional, sin FIFA, sin World Cup, sin Mundial, sin Mexico 2026, sin estadio reconocible, sin balon o zapatos con marca, sin atleta real, sin celebridad, sin UI, sin numeros, sin mapas, sin bandera, sin escudo nacional, sin piel plastica, sin caras deformes, sin manos deformes, sin dedos extra, sin flicker, sin morphing caotico, sin blur falso, sin lens flare excesivo, sin look HDR.
```
