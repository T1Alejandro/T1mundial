# Bloques Reutilizables Para Krea

Estos bloques son para pegarlos como contenido de referencia o dividirlos en secciones/sticky notes dentro del workflow. No hace falta pegarlos todos dentro de cada toma.

## Global Style

Cinematic 16:9 still frame, soft photographic tone, independent auteur cinema feeling, gentle analog film roll-off, lifted blacks, no crushed shadows, soft highlights, fine cinematic grain, natural light first, practical light second, less contrast, more atmosphere, quiet visual rhythm, slightly nostalgic but contemporary, realistic texture, no commercial stock lighting, no aggressive HDR.

## Camera / Lens

35mm or 50mm lens feeling, medium to shallow depth of field, clean legible composition, natural camera perspective, subtle haze when appropriate, no fake cinematic blur, no excessive lens flare.

## Color Continuity

Natural unsaturated greens, warm golden-hour yellows, soft cool shadows, `#DA3B2B` as red accent, dark green and warm gold only for abstract digital surfaces.

## Wardrobe Continuity

Main fictional football team: green jersey with two white graphic lines, white shorts, red socks `#DA3B2B`, no logos, no crest, no official identity.

Opposing fictional team: white jersey, blue shorts, white socks, no logos, no crest.

Referees: fluorescent green shirt, black shorts, no logos, no insignia.

Goalkeeper: solid yellow uniform, no visible identity.

## Texture Continuity

Visible tactile textures: metal, skin, fabric, cardboard, fruit, concrete, grass, dust, haze. Jerseys may have a subtle abstract geometric textile texture loosely inspired by pre-Hispanic pattern language, without copying recognizable symbols or archaeological glyphs.

## Curated Art Direction

Curated cinematic realism, not harsh documentary misery. Popular neighborhoods, small shops, markets, workshops and working people should feel dignified and intentionally art-directed: selected wardrobe, controlled palette, natural makeup, ordered set dressing and purposeful texture. Texture should feel real, clean, maintained and dignified; normal material use, not decay. No extreme poverty, abandonment, neglect, excessive grime, deep cracks, strong visual scars, humidity damage, heavy rust, peeling paint, faded broken surfaces, ruined walls as protagonist, broken/cut/collapsed elements, brutalist precarity or visual misery.

## Generic Digital Screen Treatment

All screens should display generic abstract digital surfaces. They should feel real inside the scene and be usable for later montage or adjustment. Use abstract blocks, empty fields, soft cards, simple visual hierarchy, white/red/dark green/warm gold accents when appropriate. No readable text, no numbers, no app icons, no brand marks, no final UX, no QR codes, no labels.
